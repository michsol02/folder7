SUPPORTING & REWARDING ALL BUSINESSES COMMITTED TO MAKING THE WORLD A BETTER PLACE

What if any business was able to maximise its positive impacts on people and planet, and minimise its negative impacts, to the fullest possible extent, while delivering the goods and services we all need?
What if any one of us, as a consumer, an employee or an investor, could identify those businesses and reward them with our purchases, labour and investment?

We believe the result would be profound. That is, seeking to maximise ‘net positive’ impacts on people and planet would become the profit-maximising choice for any business. And further, there’d be powerful economic incentives for all businesses to do more good, and less bad.

Responsible 100’s purpose is to support and enable any business seeking to be that choice, to empower all those who wish to identify, support and reward them, and to help create and be part of an economy that respects and safeguards the environment and serves all of society.


In August 2020 the pioneering beer company BrewDog announced it had become carbon negative, and that it was removing twice as much carbon from the air than it emits. Enabling the claim: “by drinking BrewDog beer, you're having a positive impact on the planet”. Great news for beer drinkers, and great news for the planet.
