HOW RESPONSIBLE 100 WORKS

Your Responsible 100 array will be a constantly evolving account of your responsibility challenges, performance, risks and opportunities.

We support businesses of all shapes, sizes, maturities and locations to undertake the following steps…

STEP #1

Consider the issues. Up to 58 separate responsibility issues, across up to 8 categories. This can be done by one responsible person within the company, or better by several people, and ideally with everyone in the business invited to get involved and express their views as to what is important, and what is not, for the company to be abreast of. 

STEP #2

Consider the issues. Up to 58 separate responsibility issues, across up to 8 categories. This can be done by one responsible person within the company, or better by several people, and ideally with everyone in the business invited to get involved and express their views as to what is important, and what is not, for the company to be abreast of. 

STEP #3

We’ll then present your first co-created and ordered issues heat map, with the things to worry about most on the left hand side, the things to free your mind of (at least for now) on the right, and those issues of moderate relevance or materiality in between.

STEP #4

Next up we’ll work out how you currently perform on your priority issues. We do this by helping you determine if you do anything good or bad in relation to each issue by prompting some basic disclosures - possibly involving a selection of employees or other stakeholders if that suits you - and asking follow up questions. We’ll quickly build up a picture and basic written summary of your current policies and practices, or in some cases perhaps a lack of them. These written accounts may form the basis for internal or external reporting in due course.

STEP #5

Now we can answer that most critical of questions: where should our business invest its precious resources to affect the most urgently needed change? In the below example, that would be on Reducing Social Exclusion and Corporae Culture, two top priority (or 'must report on') issues, where performance is currently POOR.

BENEFITS

In summary, Responsible 100 helps any business to:

  • IDENTIFY and prioritise the key responsibility issues to focus on, improve and innovate around
  • DETERMINE where it has the greatest opportunity to positively impact people and planet  
  • MAP out the possible pathways for achieving better social and environmental practices and outcomes 
  • CREATE comprehensive improvement plans
  • MEASURE and monitor on progress using open-sourced performance benchmarks
  • VISUALISE company performance and progress over time
  • REPORT internally and externally

Responsible 100 supports your business with every step.
